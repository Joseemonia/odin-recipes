Pasta

Description

This is a classic pasta recipe that is simple and delicious. It's perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Step 1: Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Step 2: In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Step 3: Add cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat with the garlic-infused oil.
