Cake

Description

This is a delicious homemade cake recipe that is perfect for birthdays and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Step 2: In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder.
  3. Step 3: Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
